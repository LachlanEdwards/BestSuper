Who We Are:

bestSuper Australia

Superannuation is an essential financial service offered by a range of different service providers in Australia. Superannuation providers have traditionally communicated with their members via printed material like, magazines, newsletters and brochures.

BestSuper is a superannuation provider that is shifting its member communication to an eMagazine (web-based magazine) to better target different demographic groups (“right message to to the right member at the right time.”). BestSuper has 150,000 members that currently receive a quarterly printed magazine. BestSuper has designed and built a website that implements an eMagazine that allows us to more effectively communicative with our members.
